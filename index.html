What is Excelsior?

A two-day event by IEEE SB GEC Palakkad to nurture, empower and motivate young engineers to be better at what they love through technology and innovation by providing a framework to invoke self-discovery and induce self-learning.

"Tech gives the quietest person a voice"
Jerry Blumengarten

Give yourself a voice, find the best of technology and emerge as a more pro-active and confident version of yourself.

Inspiring Keynotes

Interactive and interesting keynotes by some of the best minds in technology whose knowledge and guidance will surely be an experience like never before.

Reinventing Experiences

Stimulate the inquisitive techie in you with our events that will surely leave you reeling and rethinking all the tech talks and workshops you have ever participated in before.

Grow Your Network

What better way to have fun and eat your fill other than by mingling and networking with everyone from tech giants to curious young engineers who will grace the event with their presence!!?

About us

IEEE Student Branch
GEC Palakkad

The IEEE Student Branch of Government Engineering College Palakkad, Kerala continuously works to enhance the proficiency of each and every student in the institution as well as the locality in becoming more comfortable with enhancing technologies via talks, workshops and competitions. We work for providing an ideal platform for young minds to nourish their technical skills as an engineer by providing a technical home.
